How to make Frozen Yogurt

Description

This homemade frozen yogurt recipe is truly easy to make and it's so delicious. It's a better choice than store-bought varieties, and it tastes wonderfully fresh.

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt:
  • Sweetener: I like to use honey because it's so delicious. You can use a granulated sweetener if you prefer
  • Vanilla extract: Use high-quality pure vanilla for the best results.

    Steps

  1. Mix. You start by mixing the ingredients together. You can use a food processor, or simply do as I do in the video below and use a hand whisk.
  2. Freeze. Now transfer the mixture to a 9-inch square glass baking dish, spreading it out evenly. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the freezer for 45 minutes.
  3. Stir. Remove the pan from the freezer. Use a rubber spatula to give the mixture a good stir. You especially want to stir the already-frozen edges into the still-soft center. Flatten back, cover, and continue freezing for 2 more hours, stirring every 30 minutes.
  4. Freeze some more. The yogurt should now have a soft-serve consistency. You can go ahead and enjoy it. I usually transfer it at this point to a smaller container (this makes scooping easier), cover it, and freeze it for one more hour to achieve a scoopable consistency
  5. Enjoy
